Anchors — slot to place

Click a location below or its dot to select it, then click the board to place the active slot. Drag a dot to fine-tune. Right-click a dot or press Del to clear it.

Adjacency — toggle edges

Click location A then B to toggle the undirected edge. Click an edge line to select it, then Del to remove. Esc cancels a pending pick.
