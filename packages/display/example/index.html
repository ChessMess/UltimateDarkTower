3D Camera

The board-facing camera is defined by three factors, each a multiple of the model's radius: Elevation sets eye height, Target Height sets the look-at point (raise the board in frame by lowering it), and Distance zooms in / out of the auto-fitted framing.

Zoom to Cursor points scroll-wheel zoom at the cursor instead of the orbit centre; Preserve View On Direction Select keeps your current tilt / zoom when picking a side instead of snapping back to the fitted framing.

Drag the sliders to tune live, or orbit & zoom the 3D view by hand and click Snapshot view & copy to capture the current framing and copy the full camera config (factors plus the toggles) to your clipboard. Paste it into a CameraConfig (e.g. for UDT Board). The snapshot re-faces north — the preset always loads facing the north side.
